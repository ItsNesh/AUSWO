Explore the most requested Australian visas. We group key requirements and helpful notes to kick-start your planning.

Skilled

Subclass 189 · Skilled Independent

For highly skilled professionals without employer or state sponsorship.

  • Minimum 65 points to submit an Expression of Interest.
  • Occupation must be on the medium and long-term skills list.
  • No sponsorship required; full permanent residency.

State Nomination

Subclass 190 · Skilled Nominated

Permanent residency for applicants nominated by an Australian state or territory.

  • Occupation must be on the relevant state list.
  • Commitment to live and work in the sponsoring state.
  • 65+ points, including state nomination bonus.

Regional

Subclass 491 · Skilled Work Regional

A provisional visa for skilled migrants willing to live in regional Australia.

  • Requires nomination by a state or eligible family sponsor.
  • Five-year visa with PR pathway via subclass 191.
  • Extra points for living and working regionally.

Graduate

Subclass 485 · Temporary Graduate

Allows recent graduates to live, study, and work after finishing their studies.

  • Must have completed an eligible qualification in the last 6 months.
  • Skills assessment not required for graduate work stream.
  • Great stepping stone towards permanent residency.
